Aurum Capital is a multi-asset investment platform that lets you invest across crypto, global equities, commodities, and real estate — all from one dashboard. Deposit funds, choose your allocations, and monitor your full portfolio with real-time P&L analytics updated every 30 seconds.
Security is foundational to everything we build. Your account is protected with bank-grade 256-bit AES encryption, mandatory two-factor authentication, and cold storage for digital assets. Our platform undergoes regular third-party audits and operates under applicable financial services regulation.
You have access to four major asset classes: Cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and 50+ altcoins), Global equities (Tesla, Apple, Nvidia, Microsoft and more), Commodities (Gold, Silver, Crude Oil), and Premium real estate — fractional ownership in institutional-grade property.
There is no minimum for crypto or stock positions — you can start with any amount. Real estate investments begin from $1,000, allowing fractional access to properties that would otherwise require hundreds of thousands in capital. All minimum requirements are displayed clearly before you confirm any position.
Deposits are accepted via bank transfer, debit/credit card, and direct crypto transfer — typically credited within minutes. Withdrawals to your bank are processed within 1–3 business days; crypto withdrawals usually settle within 24 hours. There are no withdrawal fees on standard accounts.
Aurum Capital charges 0% annual management fee. A small, transparent trading spread applies to each transaction — always shown upfront before you confirm. There are no subscription fees, no hidden charges, and no penalty for holding cash in your account.
