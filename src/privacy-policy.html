Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only under the following strictly controlled circumstances:

05
// Security

Security Architecture

Aurum Capital employs a defence-in-depth approach to data security, consistent with the sensitivity of financial and personal information we hold.

  • AES-256 encryption at rest; TLS 1.3 encryption in transit for all data flows
  • Multi-factor authentication enforced across all privileged access points
  • Role-based access control with least-privilege principles and audit logging
  • SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure with annual third-party penetration testing
  • 24/7 security operations centre monitoring for anomalous activity
  • Cold storage for cryptographic assets with multi-signature institutional custody
  • Data breach incident response plan with 72-hour regulatory notification capability

02
// Asset Risks

Asset Class Specific Risks

Cryptocurrency & Digital Assets

  • Extreme price volatility — assets may lose all value within a short period
  • Regulatory risk — legislative changes may adversely impact the legality or value of digital assets
  • Custody risk — loss of private keys or exchange failure may result in permanent loss
  • Smart contract risk — code vulnerabilities may be exploited by malicious actors
  • Liquidity risk — certain tokens may be difficult or impossible to sell at a fair price

Equities

  • Market risk — general market downturns may affect all equity positions simultaneously
  • Concentration risk — sector or geographic concentration may amplify losses
  • Currency risk — foreign currency denominated positions expose you to exchange rate movements
  • Counterparty risk — the financial health of brokers and custodians remains relevant

Real Estate Investments

  • Illiquidity — property investments cannot be readily redeemed and may lock up capital for extended periods
  • Property market risk — real estate values are cyclical and may decline significantly
  • Tenant & rental risk — void periods, non-paying tenants, and property damage affect returns
  • Development risk — construction projects may face delays, cost overruns, or planning obstacles
